Meet Wendy

"AI should work for you. Not the other way around."

Based in Mexicali, Mexico, Wendy Molinares is a globally recognized AI strategist bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and human connection. Recognized by the Mexican Society for Artificial Intelligence, Wendy is renowned for her work in Artificial Empathy and her proprietary 5P’s of Prompting framework. She helps organizations navigate the complexities of automation while maintaining a deeply human core.

A bilingual educator and speaker, Wendy translates complex technical capabilities into actionable business growth, fostering an environment where technology serves as an extension of human creativity rather than a replacement for it.
